About us

Big Gaints in Industry and E-commerce currently in the market are more focused on Retailer to Consumer operations but aren't working on the problem of retailers and the Wholesaler to retailer operations.

Atulya is a perfect digital platform for B2B2C businesses. Harnessing Technology To Connect Supply Chain Partners; Atulya leverages state-of-the-art cloud technologies to help retailers manage their wholesalers and consumers so they can be more cost-efficient and responsive. A safe, trustable marketplace for wholesalers, manufacturers, and retailers gets fairer terms of trade.

Atulya helps retailers set up their B2B business with them being their customer. We are building logistics platform for transporting products between Wholesaler - Retailer which is the main problem in the cycle to create a win-win situation for retailers and consumers by bringing about transparency in pricing.
