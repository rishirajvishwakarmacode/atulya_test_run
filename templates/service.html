An Efficient Solution

Our application delivers all of the necessities of a business digitally, allowing you to run with a single screen.

Convenient Dashboard

Our Swift Dashboard allows you to quickly interact with your buyers and sellers and allows you to operate on a single screen with all of your services available.



Dynamic Inventory

Managing and viewing inventory is a time-consuming chore, but this will no longer be an issue because we have a solution for you. Buy from a wholesaler and sell to a consumer, and the product will be added or subtracted automatically based on the activity.

Live Order Management

Ordered-Shipped-Delivered, That is how we handle online orders, but we are unable to manage them properly, therefore we introduced floating order management, which allows you to manage orders straight from the dashboard.

Basic Accounting

We provide fundamental accounting services for your business, such as invoicing, GST, billing, and transaction history, which will reduce your tedious paperwork and clutter.


Realtime Analytics

Real-time statistics allow you to visualise your performance over time in terms of profit and loss, as well as consumers and demographics. Analytics may assist you in developing company plans based on consumer behaviour and, as a result, increasing your revenues.

Customer Integration

Bring your loyal customers on board with a simple invite link so they can buy with your store online, making it easier for both of you. You can even allow your reliable consumers to borrow products and pay them back later within a set time frame.
